Ramen

Description

Ramen is a Japanese noodle soup dish. It consists of Chinese-style wheat noodles served in a meat or vegetable broth, often flavored with soy sauce or miso. Ramen has various origins and is possibly the most well known dish in Japan.

Ingredients

  1. 3 ½ cups chicken broth (such as Swanson Original, Natural Goodness, or Organic Chicken Broth)
  2. 1½ red bell pepper, cut into 2-inch-long strips
  3. 1 medium carrot, sliced diagonally
  4. 2 green onions, sliced diagonally
  5. 1 stalk celery, sliced diagonally
  6. 1 teaspoon ground ginger
  7. 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  8. 1 cup cooked, shredded boneless, skinless chicken breast meat
  9. 4 ounces uncooked ramen noodles, broken into pieces

Steps

  1. Put the chicken broth, bell pepper, carrots, green onions, celery, ginger, and pepper into a large pot and bring to a simmer over medium heat.
  2. Add the chicken and cook until the meat is no longer pink, about 15 minutes.
  3. Add the ramen and cook until the noodles are tender, about 5 minutes.
